Wanna Commission Me?

Rules:

1 - Please be respectful and patient, I might be busy with college or with other commissions.

2 - You can use my art as long as you give credit.

3 - I can refuse to take commissions.

4 - Try to keep the commission talk in DMs, I will update you on how the progress is going there.

5 - Must pet the winter.

Refund policy:

You can anytime before i finish your commission.

Payment options:

Paypal (CAD).

50 50 Rule: When I complete the sketch I will send you what the commissin looks like, I will then ask for half the payment.

Once I recieve payment I will finish the commisson. When I am done I will then ask for the rest of the payment after I show you the water marked version.

This ensures your trust that I am working on your commission, and ensures my trust that you have the money to pay, since I don't do commissons for free or for followers.
